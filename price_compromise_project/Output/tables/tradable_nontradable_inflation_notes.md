# US Tradable/Nontradable Inflation Proxy, 2015-2025

Source: BLS Public Data API, CPI-U seasonally adjusted series.

- Tradable proxy: CUSR0000SACL1E, commodities less food and energy commodities.
- Nontradable proxy: CUSR0000SASLE, services less energy services.
- Annual inflation is calculated from annual average CPI indexes.
- 2025 is included as available monthly data from BLS API; check months_observed before treating it as a full year.
